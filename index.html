Word Auction

Click "Play" to start the auction. Players bid on how many words they can name from a mystery category - but the category isn't revealed until after the bidding ends! The bidder then has 30 seconds to name words. They earn 1 point for each correct word, up to their bid amount (e.g., if you bid 7 and name 10 correct words, you still only get 7 points). If they can't name at least their bid amount, they must complete a fun challenge or task.

Syllable Challenge

Click "Play" to generate a number of syllables. Players must say a word with exactly that many syllables (optionally from a category). For example, if the number is 3, valid words include "elephant" (el-e-phant), "computer" (com-pu-ter), or "beautiful" (beau-ti-ful). If they can't think of one, they must complete a fun challenge or task.
